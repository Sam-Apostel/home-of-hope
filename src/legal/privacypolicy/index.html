How will we use your data?

We collect your data so that we can:

  • Process your order.
  • Email you details on progress of your order.
  • Improve the geographical spread of our content delivery servers.
  • Make better informed decisions on the pricing of our services related to payment method overhead and shipping costs.

We will never share any of your data with any other company with commercial or analytical motifs.
We will share some of your data with a number of not predefined companies for logistical motifs.
These companies and motifs include and are confined to:

  • Companies that handle shipping, only for shipping purposes.
  • Companies that handle payment and credit reference.
  • Companies that handle our data to improve the geographical spread of our content delivery servers.

When we process your order, it may send your data to, and also use the resulting information from, credit reference agencies to prevent fraudulent purchases.

What are your data protection rights?

We would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

The right to access - You have the right to request Sam Apostel for copies of your personal data. We may charge you a small fee for this service.

The right to rectification - You have the right to request that Sam Apostel corrects any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request Sam Apostel to complete information you believe is incomplete.

The right to erasure - You have the right to request that Sam Apostel erases your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to restrict processing - You have the right to request that Sam Apostel restricts the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to object processing - You have the right to object to Sam Apostel’s processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to data portability - You have the right to request that Sam Apostel transfers the data that we have collected to another organisation, or directly to you, under certain conditions.
